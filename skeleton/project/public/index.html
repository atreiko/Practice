about

The skeleton is the structural frame that supports the animal organism. There are several different types of skeleton: an exoskeleton, which is the stable outer shell of the body, an endoskeleton, which forms a supporting structure within the body, and a hydroskeleton, a flexible skeleton supported by fluid pressure. The term comes from the Greek σκελετός (skeletos) "dried up".
